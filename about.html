Founder

From signal disorder to a clinical platform.

Justin C. Radford-Berry is the Founder and CEO of Regenesis-OS Corp. With over seven years as an independent quantitative researcher applying statistical pattern recognition and signal analysis to complex data streams, Justin identified a fundamental connection between biological signal disorder and physiological collapse.

Drawing on expertise in entropy-based pattern detection, variance analysis, and time series modeling, he developed the biological signal order framework at the core of the Regenesis-OS platform.

He holds a BA in Environmental Business from the University of Redlands and has secured access to the MIMIC-IV and eICU critical care databases for clinical research validation.
